Welcome

Life is hard, but you already know that. One moment everything is going well and then suddenly you feel the weight of the world on your shoulders. Trust me, I have been there. I have experienced grief, setbacks, questioned “why me, why now”, and felt like I could not take one more step forward. Life is hard, but life is also full of opportunity for exploration and growth, for forgiveness and empathy, for finding ourselves. I believe that is why you have chosen to seek counseling/therapy - to find your focus and drive again. And if not, let’s schedule an appointment to talk about you and what led you to consider counseling/therapy.
