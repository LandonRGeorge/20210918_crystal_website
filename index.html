Welcome

Feeling the weight of the world on your shoulders? Stuck in your career and uncertain of the next steps to take? Finding it difficult to make progress and achieve goals? Counseling is a therapeutic process where you can safely explore opportunities for growth in a non-judgmental environment.

Whether you are experiencing challenges in your professional or private life or are looking to advance in your career, this is a safe, confidential space where you can address concerns, identify and define values, establish goals and develop strategies to achieve goals. Counseling can be scary, as you are being vulnerable with a stranger, but with support you can begin taking steps forward to find acceptance and comfort. I look forward to speaking with you and discovering ways to help you achieve your goals.
